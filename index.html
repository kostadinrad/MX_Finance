Mathematical Formula Rules Applied

$\text{Variable \% Expense Rate} = \frac{\sum \text{Variable Expenses}}{\text{GGR}}$

$\text{Breakeven GGR} = \frac{\text{Total Fixed Costs} + \text{Bonus Achieve}}{1 - \text{Variable \% Expense Rate}}$

The simulation scales the variable expenses dynamically by the baseline percentage rate, holding fixed layers constant.
